# Credits

CatColab is developed by [Topos Institute](https://topos.site/) in partnership
with our collaborators, funders, and contributors from the open source
community.

## Development team

As of January 2025, the core developers of CatColab are
[Kris Brown](https://www.krisb.org/),
[Kevin Carlson](https://topos.institute/people/kevin-carlson/),
[Owen Lynch](https://owenlynch.org/), and
[Evan Patterson](https://www.epatters.org/).

Notable contributions to CatColab have also been made by Matt Cuffaro, Shaowei
Lin, and Hamidah Oderinwale. A [complete
list](https://github.com/ToposInstitute/CatColab/graphs/contributors) of
contributors is available on GitHub.

## Research

CatColab is not only a technology. It's also an ongoing research project,
drawing on research in mathematics and computer science conducted at Topos
Institute, by our collaborators, in the applied category theory community, and
beyond. An incomplete [bibliography](https://next.catcolab.org/dev/bib-0001.xml)
is available in the developer docs.
