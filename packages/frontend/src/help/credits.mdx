## Research

CatColab is not only a technology. It's also an ongoing research project,
drawing on research in mathematics and computer science conducted at Topos
Institute, by our collaborators, in the applied category theory community, and
beyond. An incomplete [bibliography](https://next.catcolab.org/dev/bib-0001.xml)
is available in the developer docs.
